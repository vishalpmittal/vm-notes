# Getting Out of a Rut

## Key Takeaways

- A rut isn't something breaking — it's the absence of choice; your defaults took over and dopamine dropped from too much sameness
- Resist the urge for drastic changes (quit job, move countries) — you'll carry the same patterns to a new setting
- Pick **one mode** (e.g., "Family-first me," "Side-project me") and commit to it for 2-3 weeks — the constraint is the point
- Build a micro-habit: once a week, do one ordinary thing differently (new cafe, swapped timing, event you'd normally skip)
- Awareness is the first lever — catch yourself on autopilot and ask "is this a choice or a leftover?"
